Easy, Delicious Breakfast Recipes

Here are 2 quick and easy ideas for a breakfast that will get you ready for the day!

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • As much cheddar cheese as you like!

Steps:

  1. First, crack your eggs into a bowl and whisk them together
  2. Put your pan on a stove turned up to medium heat
  3. Melt the butter on the pan and add the now-whisked eggs
  4. Scramble the eggs repeatedly until fluffy and no raw egg is left
  5. While scrambling the eggs, add the cheddar cheese to the pan and scramble it with the eggs
  6. Take the eggs off the pan and enjoy!

Quick, Delicious Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1 potato (any kind)
  • 1 tbsp paprika seasoning
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter

Steps:

  1. Skin and cut your potato into small, roughly 3/4 inch chunks
  2. Add paprika, salt, black pepper, and chili powder seasonings and mix until the potatoes are all coated with seasoning
  3. Micrwave the potatoes for 4 minutes so that they become soft
  4. Add your olive oil or butter a pan on medium heat
  5. Add the potatoes to the pan and cook on all sides until crispy on the outside
  6. Take the potatoes off the pan and enjoy!
